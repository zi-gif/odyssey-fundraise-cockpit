# Odyssey Fact Sheet

## Company Overview
- **Name**: Odyssey
- **Website**: odyssey.ml
- **Founded**: 2023
- **HQ**: Palo Alto, California
- **Category**: AI lab building general-purpose world models
- **Mission**: Pioneering world models to go beyond language models. Building causal, multimodal systems that learn to predict and interact with the world over long horizons.

## Product
- **Odyssey-2 Pro** (launched January 2026): Latest world model. Streams 720p interactive 3D worlds at 22 FPS. Available via developer API.
- **Core approach**: Proprietary real-world data capture using custom backpack rigs with LiDAR sensors. Models learn from physical reality, not just internet data.
- **API-first model**: Developer platform for building applications on top of world models.

## Technical Differentiation
1. **Proprietary data pipeline**: Real-world capture (LiDAR + camera backpack rigs) creates training data that cannot be replicated from internet scraping.
2. **Temporal coherence**: Models maintain consistency over extended sequences (30+ seconds), critical for interactive applications.
3. **Real-time inference**: 22 FPS at 720p enables interactive use cases (gaming, simulation, robotics).
4. **Causal understanding**: Models learn physics and causality from real-world observations, not just visual patterns.
